次元音楽和:
Tran­sient Magi­cal Cir­cus

Descri­ption

"A carnival of one night. How could THAT ever be a problem?"

次元音楽和 (Jigen Ongakuwa): Transient Magical Circus is a danmaku shoot-'em-up featuring six stages and an extra stage.

Follow the story from one of the two main characters' perspectives - Peggy Raven and Poppy Raven - in the first game in the series!

After being transported - via a mysterious, non-euclidean road - to the mythical land of Muryouriku, they come accross their first incident: A circus that's being loud and boisterous, to the chagrin of the residents of the land.

Play­able Chara­cters

Insta-shot Drama-driven Repor­ter

Name: Peggy Raven

Hability: Akin to collecting rumors

Species: Living Doll

One day, she read about a rumor online about a "mysteryous disappearing road, bewitched with eternal fog, where people enter and never return".

Thinking this might be a great opportunity for an investigation, she dragged her sister along, to the latter's dismay.

Now, stuck in another dimension, she has new matters on her hands: Start a news agency- I mean, "Find a way to return".

Picture-Perfect Fate-Woven Detec­tive

Name: Poppy Raven

Hability: Akin to solving cases

Species: Living Doll

On another day like any other - that is, without any work - she was, begrudgingly, dragged by her sister along on one of her reports, her saying something about a "mystery like no other" about a "road bewitched with eternal fog".

Now, stuck in another dimension, she has new matters on her hands: Start a detective agency- I mean, "Find a way to return".

Feat­ures

  • Six main stages + one extra stage
  • Two play­able chara­cters
